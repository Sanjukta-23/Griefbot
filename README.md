# Griefbot
 A gentle, emotionally intelligent AI companion designed to guide users through the isolating experience of grief, featuring two-way voice interaction and real-time anxiety grounding.
 
The Problem: The mental health tech space is saturated with apps for productivity, anxiety, and general stress—but grief is almost completely ignored. When someone experiences a profound loss (a loved one, a pet, a relationship, or a career), they are often left to navigate a highly non-linear, isolating journey alone.

The Solution: GriefBot provides a safe, private, and judgment-free space for users to process their emotions. Powered by Google Gemini 2.0 Flash, it acts as a highly empathetic companion that actively listens, responds with appropriate sensitivity, and guides users through their unique grief journey without offering unsolicited "medical advice." It bridges the gap between isolation and therapy.
